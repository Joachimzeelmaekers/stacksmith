# Exercise 4: Sum of Multiples

Find sum of all multiples of 3 or 5 below N.

## Approaches

- **O(N) approach:** Loop through all numbers
- **O(1) approach:** Use mathematical formula

## Key Insight

Mathematical formulas can reduce O(N) to O(1)!
